```text
Home              /
Research          /research
People            /people
Get Involved      /get-involved
```

---

# 2. Global Structure

## Header

* Lab name (Puffin Lab) + logo (on the left)
* Home
* Research
* People
* Get Involved

## Footer

* Lab name
* University and department affiliation
* Location
* Contact email
* Director’s website (https://www.zihanwu.com)
* Optional social or GitHub links

---

# 3. Home Page

## Purpose

Quickly explain who the lab is, what it studies, and how visitors can engage.

## Section Order

### 1. Hero

* Lab name
* One-sentence tagline
* University affiliation

### 2. About the Lab

A short introduction covering:

* What the lab studies
* Why the work matters
* The lab’s research approach
* The communities the lab works with
* link to meet the team and get involved

### 3. Featured Projects

Display two or three current projects.

Each project includes:

* Project title
* Two- or three-sentence description
* Research theme
* Project status
* Team member names
* Optional external link

Projects do not link to separate internal pages.

### 4. News

Display two or three short updates, such as:

* New member
* Paper acceptance
* Grant or award
* Project milestone
* Study recruitment

Each update includes:

* Date
* Headline
* Optional details/external link

---

# 4. Research Page

## Purpose

Explain the lab’s research agenda and present all current projects.

## Section Order

### 1. Research Overview

A short description of:

* The lab’s central research questions
* How the research themes connect
* The lab’s overall approach

### 2. Current Projects

Display all active projects in one list or grid.

Each project includes:

```text
Project title
Time (year - year)
Short description
Tags
A picture
Collaborators, if applicable
Funding, if applicable
Relevant links, if available
publication, if available
```

---

# 5. People Page

## Purpose

Introduce current lab members and collaborators.

## Section Order

### 1. Lab Director

Include:

* Name
* Title
* Photo
* Short biography
* Research interests
* External profile links

### 2. Current Members

Group members only when the categories are useful.

Possible categories:

* PhD Students
* Master’s Students
* Undergraduate Researchers
* Research Staff

Each member includes:

```text
Name
Photo
Role or degree program
Two- or three-sentence biography
Research interests
Current projects
Optional external links
```

### 3. Collaborators and Affiliates

Each collaborator includes:

```text
Name
Affiliation
Relationship to the lab
Associated project
External profile link
```

### 4. Join the Lab

A brief call to action linking to the Get Involved page.

Do not display empty categories such as Alumni.

---

# 6. Get Involved Page

## Purpose

Help each visitor identify the appropriate opportunity and next step.

## Section Order

### 1. Current Opportunities

Provide a brief, easy-to-update summary.

Examples:

```text
Undergraduate researchers: Open
Master’s students: Contact us
PhD recruitment: Recruiting for Fall 2027
Educator partnerships: Open
Research studies: No active recruitment
```

### 2. Current University Students

Include:

* Types of available research work
* Relevant backgrounds or interests
* Expected time commitment
* Paid, credit, independent study, or volunteer possibilities
* How to express interest
* Information to include in an inquiry

### 3. Prospective PhD Students

Include:

* Whether the lab is recruiting
* Intended admission term
* Relevant doctoral program
* Desired research alignment
* Whether applicants should contact the director
* What to include in an email
* Official program and application links

### 4. Educators and Community Partners

Include:

* Types of partnerships the lab seeks
* Example activities
* What participation may involve
* How to begin a conversation

Possible activities:

* Interviews
* Co-design workshops
* Classroom studies
* Pilot testing
* Professional development
* Collaborative grant proposals

### 5. Research Participants

When studies are actively recruiting, include:

* Study title
* Basic eligibility
* Activity
* Time commitment
* Compensation
* Online or in-person format
* Recruitment link

Hide this section when no studies are recruiting.

### 6. Research Collaborators

Include:

* Areas where collaboration is welcome
* Possible forms of collaboration
* Contact instructions
* Links to relevant research projects

### 7. Contact

* General lab email
* University and department
* Location
* Guidance on what to include in an inquiry
